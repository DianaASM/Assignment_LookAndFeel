Introduction to Saltwater Fish



Quick Info: Very hardy, easy to keep

Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae

Minimum Tank Size: 20 gallons

Diet: Omnivore

Temperament: Peaceful

Habitat: Clownfish are endemic to the warmer waters of the India Ocean. They typically live at the bottom of shallow seas in sheltered reefs or in shallow lagoons.

Additional Info: It is also known as anemonefish because they form a symbiotic mutualism with sea anemones. There are 6 mains species based on their features: Complexes, percula, tomato, skunk, clarkii, saddleback and maroon. Maroon can become aggressive in an aquarium. When a sea anemone is not available in an aquarium, the anemonefish may settle in some varieties of soft corals or large polyp stony corals
